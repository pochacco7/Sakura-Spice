    Wagyu Beef Nigiri

    Treat yourself to the indulgent flavors of our Wagyu Beef Nigiri. Each piece features thinly sliced, melt-in-your-mouth Wagyu beef delicately placed atop a bed of seasoned sushi rice, garnished with a touch of wasabi and soy sauce. Savor the rich umami taste and luxurious texture of this premium beef, perfectly complemented by the vinegared rice and subtle hints of soy. It's a culinary delight that elevates the sushi experience to new heights.

    Sip on the delicate flavors of spring with our Sakura Blossom Mocktail. This non-alcoholic drink features a blend of cherry blossom syrup, fresh lime juice, and soda water, garnished with a cherry blossom petal for an elegant and refreshing experience.
